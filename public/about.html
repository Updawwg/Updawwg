About

*What's Updawwg?*

Updawwg is a fun way to share your dog with the world. Take a picture or a video of your dog, and upload it to get started. Other users can show their appreciation for your furry friend by giving your dog an "up!"

*How do I rate other dogs?*

Access your feed and you'll see all the dogs that have been posted to Updawwg. Click on a thumbnail to see that dog's page, and give him an "up!"
